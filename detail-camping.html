Information

New Horizons Festival will have two different campsites: the Travellers Garden and the Royal Garden. This is your first stop and home for the weekend on this magical journey. Meet up with other Travellers and discover the surroundings which are beyond your imagination. The language in the Travellers Garden is music what means that no matter where you're from all Travellers will understand each other. Settle down, find some rest and look at the stars with your new made friends. The pre-set up Enjoy Tent s a spacious four person tent with four air mattresses (200 cm by 80 cm), four comfortable sleeping bags and four pillows, lights, clothing hooks, storage compartments and a separate front tent and living compartment. The tent is divided into two sleeping compartments with in between them a central baggage and living area. Specifications: 2 sleeping compartments of 180*230cm, 1 central baggage and living area of 160*230cm, ventilation and condensation system, 6 big storage compartments, Led-light and clothing hooks.

You’re able to purchase food and drinks all day and night at our 24h campsite supermarket. There will be free drinking water at the festival area and campsite near the toilets. There will be restrooms and warm water showers at the Travellers Garden, you can use them as often as you want, and there will be power outlets at designated areas at the campsite. Stay on our campsite and get an extra festival day! You can check in from Thursday, August 23rd at 12h and you won’t need to leave before Sunday, August 26th at 20h. We will host a special pre-party on Thursday (the 23th of August 2018) for all those Travellers staying at the campsite. Arriving on Thursday? Make sure to be prepared for a crazy night full of fun! The pre-party will be on the festival site and the location will be announced upfront on our website and socials. Come to New Horizons in a relaxed way without bringing a tent. Your New Horizons tent will be ready at your arrival.
