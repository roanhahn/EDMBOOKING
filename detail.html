Hotel information

The Campanile hotel is 4.5 km from the city centre of ‘s-Hertogenbosch and within 5 km of a karting circuit, tennis courts, a skating rink, swimming pool, snooker hall, a nightclub and a shopping centre.

The soundproof rooms are modern and tastefully decorated with comfortable beds, a private bathroom with shower, bath and hair dryer, ecological shampoo and soap, free Wi-Fi, a flat screen TV with free channels, blackout curtains and a free tray with coffee and tea facilities and biscuits. In the morning the hotel serves a breakfast buffet which includes tea, coffee, fresh fruit juice, cereals, pastries, cheese, meat, yoghurt and fruit salad.

Campanile Hotel also offers a hot breakfast with eggs cooked just the way you like them. The restaurant serves lunch and dinner (International dishes and Dutch specialties). The hotel also offers packed lunches, a bar/lounge, terrace, garden and self-serve laundry.
